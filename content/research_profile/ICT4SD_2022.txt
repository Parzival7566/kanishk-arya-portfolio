- "A Review of the Applications and Future Scope of Artificial Intelligence in Smart Transport" (August 2022)

Accomplishments: 
This paper provided a comprehensive review of the burgeoning field of AI in smart transportation. I explored various applications, ranging from autonomous vehicles and traffic optimization to predictive maintenance and passenger safety. I also delved into the future scope of AI in this domain, discussing emerging trends and potential challenges. The paper was presented at the ICT4SD 2022 conference held in Panjim, Goa, and subsequently published in "ICT Infrastructure and Computing."

Skills Gained: 
This research experience honed my literature review and academic writing skills. I learned to synthesize information from diverse sources, critically analyze research findings, and present my arguments in a clear and concise manner. The presentation and publication process further developed my communication and collaboration skills.
